About me

Hi, I'm Maxine.

I help kids' studios stay close to their families through warm, done-for-you parent newsletters. I take the whole thing off your plate — the writing, the design, the layout — so you can focus on teaching while your community stays engaged and excited.

I'm a marketing writer and designer by training. After years helping brands find their voice, I realised the people who need that most aren't big companies — they're the small, owner-run studios doing wonderful work who just don't have time to tell their story. So I started doing it for them.
